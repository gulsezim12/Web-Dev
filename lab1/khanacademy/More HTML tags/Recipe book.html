Pizza

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves:3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cup
Water 1 cup
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Mazarella cheese 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil in a bowl.
  2. Knead the dough until it becomes soft and elastic.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Spread tomato sauce on the dough and add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for 15–20 minutes.
